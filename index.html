Safety Rules

  1. Wear all provided safety gear at all times.
  2. Follow staff instructions and orientation.
  3. No open-toe shoes; closed-toe footwear required.
  4. Do not swing at other participants or staff.
  5. Report any injuries or hazards immediately.
  6. Only smash approved items inside designated zones.
